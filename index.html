Open Source Awards

Open Source software is central to global tech development, driven by a culture of sharing and transparency. GitNation champions these achievements, supporting OSS projects and celebrating them at the annual OS Awards. As an open foundation, we invite collaboration with individuals and businesses to expand Open Source culture. Contact us at hi@gitnation.org to join our initiative.
